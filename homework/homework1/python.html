Python is a high level general-purpose programming language. It was released in 1991. The language. supports many different paradigms including object-oriented, functional, imperative and procedural programming. It's designed to look more like the English language and eliminates most punctuation. Since 2003, it has ranked in the Top 10 Most Popular Programming languages.
